## What "delta" means here

A delta-pair is a pair of World Cup markets where the implied conditional probability
between them diverges from what would be structurally expected (e.g., a later-stage
market priced higher than an earlier-stage market for the same team). Delta magnitude
does **not** mean the market is wrong. Thin books, timing differences, and genuine
public disagreement all produce deltas.

## Implied-probability limitations

The delta computation uses mid-price (average of bid and ask). Thin books produce wide
spreads and misleading mid-prices. Always check order-book depth before acting on a
delta signal. A $50 book at a given price is not the same as a $50,000 book.
